Passphrase Finder

Passphrase Finder will attempt to recover a missing passphrase for BIP39 or BIP44 wallets.

  • You need a valid seed phrase for the wallet
  • You need a receive address from the wallet
  • You need a list of possible passphrases (for BIP39) or a tokenlist (for Ethereum validator)
  • Finding the correct passphrase is difficult and may take a long time
  • This tool cannot find a hardware wallet PIN
